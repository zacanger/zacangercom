What I Do

My music ranges all over the place. I started out playing a drum kit, then moved to guitar, and eventually learned to play bass, sing, and play a bit of piano. In 2005 I started recording my music, and have self-recorded and released almost 30 albums since then. As of June 2013, Sugo Music Group is distributing a large portion of my back catalog. I usually work with indie, rock, various styles of electronica, new age, fingerstyle acoustic guitar, neo-classical, and sludge-rock. No single word could really describe what kind of music I write and record.

I've released music using the monikers Zac Anger, Zac Anger and the Heretics, The Left Project, and g|!t©H.

I've also played in several bands in the York and Lancaster PA area. I've drummed for The Snails, played bass in The Mayor's Wife, guitar with Past Forward and served various roles with Hayley Jane and Electric Mime Symphony.
